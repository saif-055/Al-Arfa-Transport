Al Arfa General Transport is a trusted UAE-based logistics and transportation company.

Our mission is to deliver seamless transportation solutions by combining operational excellence with a commitment to customer satisfaction.

For broad transport coverage across industries, Al Arfa General Transport serves a wide range of sectors. Our commitment to excellence, a highly skilled team, and smart logistics tools ensures quality, reliable delivery every time. All vehicles undergo regular mechanical inspections and are equipped with GPS tracking for safe and real-time monitored shipments.

The team at Al Arfa General Transport brings years of combined experience in general freight, heavy equipment hauling, construction material delivery, and fleet‑based transportation services. Our staff is composed of skilled logistics professionals dedicated to creating efficient solutions tailored to each client’s needs.

Our deep understanding of the transport landscape across the UAE allows us to solve complex delivery challenges. We don’t just move goods — we build long‑term trust through clarity, reliability, and dedication.

As a locally operated logistics provider, Al Arfa General Transport is committed to offering practical, results‑driven transport solutions. While many companies stop at pickup and delivery, we ensure your goods are handled with care and delivered with precision and accountability.

We go the extra mile to ensure client satisfaction — from first mile to last mile.

With a strong commitment to your success, Al Arfa offers scalable transportation options to meet both routine and urgent delivery requirements. No matter the size, type, or frequency of your shipment, our team is always ready to make sure your logistics run smoothly.
